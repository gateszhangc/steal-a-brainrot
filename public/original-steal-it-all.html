Gameplay Guide To Steal It All

Getting Started

In Steal It All, players begin their journey by creating a unique character that reflects their style. The game offers a tutorial mode, allowing newcomers to familiarize themselves with the mechanics of the game. During this phase, players learn how to navigate the colorful world, interact with other players, and grasp the essentials of stealing cars, setting the stage for an exciting adventure.

Stealing Cars

Once you're comfortable with the basics, it's time to dive into the thrilling aspect of stealing cars. Players must sneak around and use stealth to approach vehicles owned by others. The more successful heists a player executes, the more powerful they become in the game. With a variety of unique cars available, each with distinct attributes, players must choose wisely to optimize their gameplay and enhance their car collection.

Building Your Empire

As players accumulate cars, they can start building their empire and earning passive income. This aspect of the game encourages strategic investment in upgrades that enhance earnings and expand their collection. Additionally, players need to devise defensive strategies to protect their cars from rival players. Setting up defenses and traps is crucial for safeguarding assets and maintaining dominance in the chaotic world of Steal It All.

Upgrades And Rebirth

Progressing through the game allows players to unlock insane upgrades through a rebirth mechanic. This feature not only boosts abilities but also enhances the overall gameplay experience. Players can further engage in the hilarity of the game by utilizing trolling mechanics, such as slap power and troll gear, to mess with other players, adding an extra layer of fun and unpredictability to their adventures.
